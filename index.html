Our Story

It all started with a counter-intuitive product: a corporate card with spend management software that actually helps companies spend less of their two most valuable resources: time and money.

Founded in 2019 and backed by leading investors including Founders Fund, Stripe, Goldman Sachs, Coatue Management, D1 Capital Partners, Redpoint Ventures and Thrive Capital, Ramp was recently named Fast Company’s #1 Most Innovative Company in North America.

We are NYC’s fastest growing startup and America’s fastest growing corporate card. We’re building the ultimate platform for modern finance teams – and we’re just getting started.
